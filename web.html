What is Web Developer

An expert who focuses on building and managing websites is known as a web developer. Website structure, appearance, and functionality are created using a combination of programming languages, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. A website's front end, which users interact with, and back end, which includes database management and server-side scripting, are both worked on by web developers. They have to make sure websites work well on different devices and browsers and are responsive and easy to use. To creat e cutting-edge and effective online solutions, web developers also frequently keep up with emerging technology and industry best practices.
